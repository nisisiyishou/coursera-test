Chicken

Chicken meat is a white meat, distinguished from other meats such as beef and lamb by its lower iron content (0.7 mg vs. 2 mg/100 g). Breast meat contains less than 3 g of fat/100 g. An average value for red meat (without skin) is 5 to 7 g/100 g.

Beef

Beef, flesh of mature cattle, as distinguished from veal, the flesh of calves. The best beef is obtained from early maturing, special beef breeds. High-quality beef is firm, velvety, fine-grained, lean, bright red in colour and well-marbled.

Sushi

Sushi, a staple rice dish of Japanese cuisine, consisting of cooked rice flavoured with vinegar and a variety of vegetable, egg, or raw seafood garnishes and served cold.
